About This Website

Purpose

This website was created to address the challenge of tracking the performance and holdings of covered call ETFs in detail. It provides clear, interactive dashboards and additional analysis to help users understand their investments at a deeper level.

What You Can Do

  • View up-to-date and historical ETF holdings and weights.
  • Analyze covered call options and calculate potential upside and forgone gains.
  • Interact with dashboards by updating underlying prices to see immediate effects.

Data Sources & Accuracy

The data displayed comes from publicly available sources and is provided for informational purposes only. While we strive for accuracy, we do not guarantee the completeness or timeliness of the information. Any reliance on the data is at your own risk.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to monitor site usage and improve functionality. No personal data is shared outside of anonymized analytics.

About the Creator

This site is maintained by a data enthusiast passionate about providing clear, actionable insights for investors. It is built to make ETF and options analysis accessible and intuitive.
